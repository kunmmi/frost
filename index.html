how to buy?

1

Download the MetaMask extension from the official website and install it in your browser. Fund it by transferring Based Ethereum from an exchange or alternative on-ramp and swap it to BASE chain.

2

Go to Uniswap's website and connect your Metamask wallet.

3

To swap Base for $frost, select BASE in the "from" field and choose $bonk in the "to" field using our contract address. Enter the amount of tokens you want & press swap.

4

Verify your transaction status in MetaMask under the "Activity" tab or on Basescan using your Base address. You import the token to your MetaMask by pasting in the contract address "CA".
